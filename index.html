Why Auréline Exists

Jewellery with Meaning

Auréline was created from the belief that some moments deserve more than a photograph. They deserve something you can carry with you every day.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can permanent jewellery be removed?

Yes. It can be safely cut with small scissors or jewellery cutters whenever you choose.

Can I travel through airport security?

Permanent jewellery is generally fine for everyday travel and airport security.

Can I swim, shower or exercise with it?

You can wear it during normal daily activities. Aftercare guidance will be provided at your appointment.

What happens if the weld breaks?

Please contact Auréline so we can assess the piece and advise on rewelding.

Do private bookings have a minimum?

Yes. Private bookings require a minimum of two guests.
